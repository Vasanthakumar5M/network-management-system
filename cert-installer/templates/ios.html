iOS Certificate Installation

Follow these steps to install the security certificate on your iPhone or iPad

1 Download
2 Install Profile
3 Trust Certificate
4 Complete

Step 1: Download Certificate

Tap the button below to download the security certificate profile.

Download Certificate
After downloading, you'll see a message saying "Profile Downloaded". Tap Close to continue.

Step 2: Install the Profile

  1. Open Settings

    Go to your iPhone/iPad Settings app

  2. Find Profile Downloaded

    You'll see "Profile Downloaded" near the top. Tap on it.

  3. Tap Install

    Tap "Install" in the top right corner

  4. Enter Passcode

    Enter your device passcode when prompted

  5. Confirm Install

    Tap "Install" again to confirm, then tap "Done"

Step 3: Trust the Certificate (Important!)

This step is required! The certificate won't work without enabling trust.
  1. Open Settings > General

    Go to Settings, then tap General

  2. Tap "About"

    Scroll down and tap "About"

  3. Tap "Certificate Trust Settings"

    Scroll to the bottom and tap "Certificate Trust Settings"

  4. Enable Trust

    Find "{{ config.company_name }}" and toggle the switch ON

  5. Tap Continue

    Tap "Continue" to confirm

Installation Complete!

You're all set!

The security certificate has been successfully installed on your device. You can now browse securely on this network.
